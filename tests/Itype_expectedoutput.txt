1️⃣ addi x10, x0, 12

Operation: x10 = x0 + 12 = 0 + 12

Result: x10 = 12

ALU flags: Zero = 0, cf = ?, sf = 0, vf = ? (not critical here)

2️⃣ andi x11, x10, 0xF

Operation: x11 = x10 & 0xF = 12 & 15

Binary: 12 = 1100, 15 = 1111

Bitwise AND: 1100 & 1111 = 1100 = 12

Result: x11 = 12

3️⃣ ori x12, x10, 0x3

Operation: x12 = x10 | 0x3 = 12 | 3

Binary: 12 = 1100, 3 = 0011

Bitwise OR: 1100 | 0011 = 1111 = 15

Result: x12 = 15

4️⃣ xori x13, x10, 5

Operation: x13 = x10 ^ 5 = 12 ^ 5

Binary: 12 = 1100, 5 = 0101

XOR: 1100 ^ 0101 = 1001 = 9

Result: x13 = 9

5️⃣ slti x14, x10, 20

Operation: x14 = (x10 < 20) ? 1 : 0 (signed)

12 < 20 → true

Result: x14 = 1

6️⃣ sltiu x15, x10, 5

Operation: x15 = (x10 < 5) ? 1 : 0 (unsigned)

12 < 5 → false

Result: x15 = 0

7️⃣ ebreak

This is a system instruction. It halts the CPU or triggers a debug exception, so no register changes.
